ABOUT

Liah Cheston earned her Bachelor of Arts in both Sound Engineering as well as Music Business Management at Columbia College in Chicago, IL in 2001. During her studies at Columbia College she was also producing and mixing different music projects at Studio Chicago and 79th Street. In 2001 Liah Cheston started mastering at Cosmos Mastering were she stay briefly until she moved on to Cutting Room in 2002, both based in Stockholm, Sweden. During the coming years she became involved building bridges within the music industry with countries in eastern Europe and also been a guest speaker through the Swedish Trade Council at conventions. In 2005 she got the opportunity to build a mastering company called Chartmakers OY in Helsinki, Finland. In 2009 she came back to Cutting Room and one year later she created Mediacord Mastering, a subsidiary of Cutting Room. The idea of Mediacord Masering was to create an after hour mastering service with the concept that “everyone should be able to afford good quality mastering”. In 2011 the service became an extended part of the Cutting Room Studios. Today Liah Cheston is a freelance mastering engineer, guest speaker and creative consultant within the music industry. “With over 10 years of experience, she is one of the few female master engineers in the business.”
